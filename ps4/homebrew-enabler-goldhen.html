GoldHEN

GoldHEN — the homebrew enabler

Once a jailbreak has run, GoldHEN provides the environment you use: a homebrew menu, plugin support, and app/emulator loading. It depends on the jailbreak being active, so it is loaded each session after the exploit. Keep to the latest release.

Remember

Nothing is permanent — after a reboot you re-run the jailbreak and re-load GoldHEN.
