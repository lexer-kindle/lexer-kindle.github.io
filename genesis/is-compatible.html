Is my Genesis compatible?

Is My Genesis Compatible?

Almost every Genesis or Mega Drive can be modified in some way, but which upgrades make sense depends on your board revision. Use the table below as a quick reference, then confirm your exact model on the Identifying your model page.

ModelFlashcartRGB outputNotes
Model 1 (early)YesExcellentBest RGB source; some have a wide slot fitting JP carts
Model 1 (later)YesExcellentNarrower slot; widening helps for imports
Model 2YesGoodSmaller, common, stereo via headphone-era boards varies
Model 3YesRGB capableOutputs RGB but audio is mono only; no add-on support

Add-on compatibility

The Sega CD and 32X attach to Model 1 and Model 2 consoles. The Model 3 does not support either add-on, and it also lacks an expansion port, so it is the worst choice if you want a full classic stack.

Quick takeaway

If video quality and add-on support matter most, a Model 1 is the strongest all-around platform. If you only want a flashcart for cartridge games, any model works fine.

Flashcarts like the Mega EverDrive Pro and Mega SD work across all three models, so SD-based gaming is universally available.
